Senzu Beans

Mystical beans with immense rejuvenation properties!

Senzu Beans are grown by Korin in Korin Tower. When eaten, a person's physical condition
is near-instantly restored to its natural peak.
This property makes them immensely useful to carry in battle.

Ingredients

  • A handful of senzu beans
  • A major injury

Instructions

  1. Suffer a catastrophic injury
  2. Pop a handful of Senzu Beans
  3. Return to peak physical form
